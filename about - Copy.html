Welcome to Kushi Construction

4 Years Experience

Khushi Construction is a trusted name in stone-based construction services across Maharashtra, Gujarat, and India. With years of experience, we deliver durable and eco-friendly structures such as Gabion Structures, Check Dams, Rock Fill Dams, Loose Boulder Structures, Stone Pitching, and Compound Walls.

We focus on quality workmanship, timely project completion, and sustainable techniques that help in water conservation, soil erosion control, and rural development. Our team is committed to providing cost-effective and long-lasting solutions for government projects, private contracts, and community needs.

Frequently Asked Question

You May Ask

What areas do you provide services in?
We provide construction services across Maharashtra, Gujarat, and other parts of India.
What type of construction work do you specialize in?
We specialize in Gabion structures, check dams, compound walls, stone pitching, and other stone-based construction projects.
How long does it take to complete a check dam project?
The duration depends on project size, but most check dams are completed within 2–3 months.
Do you use eco-friendly construction methods?
Yes, we focus on sustainable and eco-friendly techniques that help in water conservation and soil protection.
Can you handle both small and large projects?
Absolutely. We work on projects ranging from small compound walls to large-scale gabion structures.
How can I request a project estimate?
You can contact us directly via phone or the contact form on our website to get a free estimate.
What materials do you use in your projects?
We use high-quality stones, cement, and eco-friendly materials suitable for long-lasting construction.
Why should we choose Khushi Construction?
We provide reliable, durable, and cost-effective construction solutions with years of experience in the industry.
Do you provide maintenance after construction?
Yes, we also offer maintenance and repair services for structures like dams, walls, and stone works.
Do you provide on-site consultation before starting the project?
Yes, our team visits the site, inspects the location, and then provides the best possible construction plan.
