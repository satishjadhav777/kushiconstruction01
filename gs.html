Gabion Structure

Gabion structures are wire mesh boxes filled with stones or rocks, used for erosion control, slope stabilization, retaining walls, and water conservation. They are cost-effective, eco-friendly, and highly durable. Gabions allow natural water drainage while providing strong support against soil erosion.

Benefits:

  • Prevents soil erosion and landslides
  • Cost-effective and long-lasting
  • Environment-friendly structure
  • Improves groundwater recharge
